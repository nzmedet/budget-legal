FAQ

Budget. is a simple spending tracker. You can quickly add spendings, review day/week/month lists, analyze charts, and view totals by category.

What does Budget. do?

Budget. helps you track spendings and understand where your money goes using timeline views, charts, and category summaries.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. Budget. is local-first and stores spending data on your device.

Can I remove entries?

Yes. You can delete spending entries directly in the app.

Can I see spending trends over time?

Yes. Use chart view for monthly trends and switch between day, week, and month timeline views.

How can I get support?

Open the Support page and use the Contact Support button.
