Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MEDET TLEUGABYLULY LIMITED ("Company", "we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the Budget. app.

Summary

  • Budget. is designed for local usage on your device.
  • Budget. does not require account registration.
  • Budget. does not upload your spending data to our cloud servers.

Data We Process

Budget. stores your spending records, categories, and related budget information locally on your device so the app can function.

We do not intentionally collect personal profile data such as your name, phone number, or email through the app itself.

How Your Data Is Used

Your locally stored data is used only to provide app features such as adding spendings, viewing section lists, and reviewing your budget history.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Because app spending data is stored locally, we do not routinely receive that data and do not share it with third parties.

Data Retention

Your budget data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or remove the app. If you uninstall the app, local app data may be deleted by your operating system.

Children's Privacy

Budget. is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can edit or delete entries directly in the app.
  • You can remove app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact and Support

For privacy questions, use the support instructions on the Support page.

If you do not agree with this policy, you should discontinue use of the app.
